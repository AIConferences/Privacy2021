Registration

Please send an email to AI4MED2021@easychair.org to register and receive the information for the Zoom-online conference!

Registration for AI4MED 2021 is free through support from Taylor & Francis Group.

At least one author per accepted paper must register to the conference by Monday, 7th August 2021.

The conference will be implemented using Zoom.

Detailed information about how to access the virtual conference will be sent to the registered participants ahead of the conference.
